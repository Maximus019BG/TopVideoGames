Minecraft

Minecraft tournaments are competitions held within the Minecraft game, usually involving a group of players competing against each other in various challenges or games. These tournaments can take many forms, including PvP battles, parkour challenges, building competitions, and mini-games.

Some popular Minecraft tournaments include the Minecraft Championship, the MINECRAFT Survival Games, and the Minecraft Hunger Games. These tournaments often feature wellknown Minecraft content creators and attract large audiences on streaming platforms such as Twitch and YouTube.

Players often compete for prizes or bragging rights, and the competition can be intense. Minecraft tournaments provide a fun and engaging way for players to showcase their skills and creativity while connecting with other members of the Minecraft community.

League of Legends

Tournaments in League of Legends (LoL) refer to organized competitive events where professional or amateur teams compete against each other for prizes, glory, and recognition.

The most prestigious tournament in LoL is the annual League of Legends World Championship, which features the best teams from all around the world. Other major tournaments include the Mid-Season Invitational and the All-Star Event.

In addition to these global events, there are also regional tournaments, such as the League Championship Series (LCS) in North America, the League of Legends European Championship (LEC) in Europe.

Smaller tournaments are also held throughout the year, often organized by third-party organizers or sponsors.

Rocket League

Tournaments in Rocket League are organized competitive events where players or teams compete against each other for prizes, recognition, and the chance to prove their skills on a global stage.

Rocket League has a thriving competitive scene, with several tournaments and leagues available to players of all skill levels. The most prestigious event is the annual Rocket League Championship Series (RLCS), which features the best teams from around the world competing for a prize pool of hundreds of thousands of dollars.

In addition to the RLCS, Rocket League has several other major tournaments, including the Rocket League Championship Series Seasonal Championships, the Rocket League Summit, and the Rocket League X Games.

Assassin's Creed

Assassin's Creed is a popular action-adventure video game series, but it is not typically known for organized competitive tournaments.

While there have been some small-scale community-run events, such as speedrunning competitions or challenge runs, Assassin's Creed is primarily a single-player game, and its gameplay is focused on exploration, stealth, and narrative-driven experiences rather than competitive play.

That said, the series has occasionally featured multiplayer modes in some of its games, such as Assassin's Creed Brotherhood and Assassin's Creed Unity. These multiplayer modes allowed players to compete against each other in various challenges and modes, but they were not typically organized into formal tournaments.

Star Wars Battlefront

Star Wars Battlefront tournaments are competitive events where players can showcase their skills and compete against others to win prizes or recognition. These tournaments can take place online or in person, and can be organized by individuals, communities, or gaming companies.

Once the tournament begins, players compete against each other in a series of matches, with the winner advancing to the next round. The tournament usually culminates in a championship match, where the top players compete for the grand prize.

There are a variety of different types of Star Wars Battlefront tournaments, including team-based tournaments, solo tournaments, and objective-based tournaments. Some tournaments may also feature special rules or restrictions, such as limiting the use of certain weapons or vehicles.

GTA V

There are several GTA 5 tournaments that take place online and offline, hosted by various organizations and gaming communities.

ESL GTA 5 Online Series - The Electronic Sports League (ESL) hosts regular online tournaments for GTA 5 players. These tournaments feature different game modes and challenges, and the winners can earn cash prizes.
Red Bull Rise Till Dawn - Red Bull hosts a unique GTA 5 tournament called Rise Till Dawn, which takes place in New York City. The tournament is a 12-hour overnight event where players compete in various challenges and games, with the winner receiving a cash prize.
GTA World Championship - The GTA World Championship is a massive online tournament hosted by the World Gaming Network. It features multiple stages and challenges, and the final winner can receive a prize pool of up to $100,000.

Warzone 2 world series

In Warzone 2 there are a lot of tournaments: community tournaments; tournaments from the developers.

One tournament called "World series of Warzone 2" is a developers tournament. The trio tournament is one of the biggest ever with a price pool of over $1.2 million and in-game rewards.

Rules: Every kill is a point; 1st place = 2 x points ; 2nd - 5th place = 1.8 x points; 6th - 10 th place = 1.6 x points; 11th - 20th place = 1.4 x points; 21th - 35th = 1.2 x points; 36th - 50th place 1 x points. There are 3 stages and two regions EU and NA. Stage 1: in-game open, qualifiers and regional finales for EU. Stage 2 in-game open, qualifiers and regional finales for NA. Stage 3 final. Top 25 from stage 1 wins a money prize (prize pool $150,000) continues to the next stage and the 15 from regional finals of stage 2 win a money prize (prize pool $150,000) and go to stage 3. The winners of the whole tournament win $600,000.

Counter Strike (CS)

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a popular first-person shooter game and has a thriving esports scene with many tournaments taking place around the world. Here are some of the most significant CS:GO tournaments:

ESL Pro League: It is a professional esports league with teams from Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific regions. It features two seasons per year, and the finals are held at different locations around the world. Intel Extreme Masters: It is a series of international esports tournaments sponsored by Intel. It features the world's best players and teams competing in different countries for prize money and championship titles. Major Championships: These are the most prestigious events in the CS:GO tournament scene, organized by Valve Corporation. The tournament features the top 24 teams from around the world competing for a $1,000,000 prize pool and the title of Major Champions.
